**Case Study 2: E-commerce SEO Success**

**Overview:**
An established e-commerce brand approached CutCraft to improve their organic search performance. We implemented a comprehensive SEO strategy to boost search rankings and drive quality traffic.

**Challenges:**

*   Low organic visibility

*   Outdated website content

*   Limited backlink profile

**Our Approach:**

*   Conducted a thorough SEO audit and competitor analysis

*   Optimized on‑page elements (meta tags, headings, and content)

*   Developed a robust backlink strategy and content marketing plan

**Results:**

*   300% increase in organic traffic

*   Top 3 rankings for multiple high‑value keywords

*   50% improvement in overall conversion rates
