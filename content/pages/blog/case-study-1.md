**Case Study 1: Retail ROI Boost with PPC Campaigns**

**Overview:**
CutCraft partnered with a national retail brand to optimize their pay‑per‑click (PPC) strategy. Our targeted ad campaigns and data-driven approach helped the client reach the right audience and maximize return on investment.

**Challenges:**

*   High cost per acquisition

*   Low conversion rates

*   Inefficient ad spend across multiple channels

**Our Approach:**

*   Conducted in‑depth keyword research and audience segmentation

*   Optimized ad copy and landing pages for higher conversion

*   Utilized A/B testing to refine targeting and bids

**Results:**

*   250% increase in ROI

*   40% reduction in cost per click

*   3‑fold improvement in conversion rates

**Client Testimonial:**
*"CutCraft’s PPC expertise transformed our digital advertising. We now see a significant return on every dollar spent."*
